Information We Collect

From you directly:

  • Personally identifiable information including your name and billing information such as credit card numbers.

  • Profile information such as username, password, subscription history, preferences, feedback, and survey responses.

Automatically collected through the Services:

  • Information about your usage patterns, including IP addresses, browser type and language, referring and exit pages, URLs, date and time of access, duration of time spent on specific pages, and the features of the Services you use or visit.
  • Activity data such as watchlists, ticker searches, stock scanner usage, AI analysis views, news catalyst views, and similar activities conducted through the Services.
  • Tracking data gathered using first and third-party cookies, pixels, web server logs, web beacons, and similar technologies. This may include information such as IP address, browser type, ISP, platform type, and device type.

When you use our services, your personal information may be collected and used in the following scenarios:

  • When you create a Money Making Moves user account using your email address, we will collect this contact information.
  • We may send emails or notifications to you regarding your subscription, account activity, or product updates. You may unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time.

How We Use Your Personal Information

  • To provide our services to you, including access to the stock scanner, AI analysis, watchlists, news catalysts, and subscription features.
  • To develop and improve our Services.
  • To send notifications regarding our products and services.
  • To send promotions and other personalized information to you.
  • To process subscription payments and manage your account.
  • To evaluate and improve the effectiveness of our promotions.
  • To invite you to take surveys for the purpose of assessing our products.

Third-Party Service Providers

Money Making Moves engages third-party service providers to perform certain functions, including payment processing via Stripe, database management via Supabase, and analytics. We may share your personal information with these service providers on the condition that they use your personal data only on our behalf and pursuant to our instructions.

  • Legal compliance: We may disclose your information to comply with a law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Protection of rights and safety: We may disclose your information to protect the rights, interests, safety, and security of Money Making Moves, our users, or the public.

Opt-Out Option

If you do not wish to have your information shared with third-party companies for marketing purposes, you have the option to opt-out by contacting us at support@moneymakingmoves.com. Please allow approximately 30 days from our receipt of your request for your instructions to become effective.

How We Protect Your Information

Money Making Moves employs appropriate electronic and procedural safeguards to protect information under our control from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. No security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee complete security. We strongly suggest that you use a strong password and perform all actions in a secure internet environment.

We have established a data breach response plan. If there is a data breach involving the personal information of our users, we will take appropriate steps to contain the breach and notify affected users. Please contact us at support@moneymakingmoves.com if you believe your account security has been compromised.

Money Making Moves retains your personal information for as long as necessary to accomplish the purpose for which it was collected or as required by applicable laws.

Accessing Your Personal Information

If you are a registered user, we provide you with tools and account settings to access, correct, delete, or modify your personal information. If you believe we might have any information from a minor, please contact us at support@moneymakingmoves.com.

California Residents (CCPA)

Subject to certain limitations and exceptions, the CCPA provides California residents the following rights. Please note we do not sell personal information. California residents may exercise their rights by contacting us at support@moneymakingmoves.com.

  • Right to know: You may request details about the personal information we collect about you. We are required to respond to up to two requests per year, free of charge.
  • Right to request deletion: You may request deletion of personal information we have collected from you.
  • Right to opt out of sale: We do not sell your personal information. We will not discriminate against California residents who exercise their privacy rights.
  • Shine the Light Act: You may request information about how we share your information with third parties for marketing use.
  • Do Not Track: We do not currently respond to web browser "do not track" signals.

Nevada Residents

We do not sell consumer covered information for monetary consideration as defined in Chapter 603A of the Nevada Revised Statutes. Nevada residents may submit a request directing us not to sell their personal information by contacting us at support@moneymakingmoves.com.

European and United Kingdom Residents (GDPR)

If you are a resident of the EEA or UK, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access, correct, update, or request deletion of your personal information at any time.
  • The right to opt out of marketing communications by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any marketing email.
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time where we collected your information based on consent.
  • The right to data portability — to receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • The right to complain to a data protection authority about our collection and use of your personal information.
